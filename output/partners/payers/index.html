Payers

Revolutionize Member Engagement.

CareMessageTM enables payers to create meaningful conversations with their members around topics that are important to them.

Connect

Create automated, two-way text message conversations on any number of topics, allowing you to have a personal conversation with thousands of your members at once.

Measure

Seamlessly survey your members to collect and analyze relevant data, such as health risk assessments (HRAs) and risk-adjustment data.

Educate

Leverage our evidence-based health coaching programs to incentivize healthy behaviors and proper disease self-management.

Empower

Offer your members free tools that can help them better self-manage their care, such as automated medication adherence and glucose tracking reminders.

Engage

Use our powerful analytics engine, enabling you to directly quantify member engagement. Optimize your outreach for important factors like frequency, timing, complexity, and tonality.

Integrate

Diversify your communication capabilities by seamlessly integrating our messaging and content management capabilities into your existing technologies.
