SugarTalk and PlaticaDulce are year long programs that comprise shorter 4-6 week long modules addressing topics most relevant to type 2 diabetics - such as properly injecting insulin, recognizing a high or low blood glucose or avoiding culturally-specific foods that tend to increase blood glucose. The programs are tailored to various factors, including disease progression, age, gender, ethnicity and cultural preferences.

Heart2Heart and DeCorazón are year long programs that, like our diabetes programs, cover topics most relevant to hypertensives - such as checking blood pressure, reducing sodium intake, increasing exercise or properly taking medication. The programs are meant to help support and motivate patients as they make lifestyle changes.

Programs handcrafted for your patient.

Patient Attitude

Address cultural barriers to accepting a diagnosis, and help patients self-actualize and understand factors that motivate them to change.

Medical Terms, Simplified

Enable patients to understand difficult medical concepts, such as the relationship between glucose and insulin.

Test Results

Help patients understand the significance of their test results, such as HbA1C and cholesterol, and remind them when they're due for their next checkup.

Self Management

Teach patients to self-manage various aspects of their condition, such as conducting regular foot exams at home.

Remote Monitoring

Remind patients to measure blood glucose and other metrics regularly, and allow them to text and store these numbers in a secure database.

Flexible Engagement

Patients can be enrolled in the entire year long program, or any of the shorter 4-6 week long modules.

Our Design Process

We believe that addressing healthcare is a multi-stage problem solving process that needs to put the user at the center of our thinking. Before we can make an impact, we need to understand.

  • Phase I
  • Phase II
  • Phase III

Interview

We gain an understanding of our users through in-depth interviews with all stakeholders, including patients, physicians, health educators, care coordinators etc.

Observe

We observe, document and evaluate patient visits, group meetings, diabetes classes, one-on-one health education appointments etc.

Validate

We complement our findings with clinically-proven interventions and techniques, such as motivational interviewing and values based affirmation.

Create

We take the immense quantity of data generated in Phase I to outline specific programs. These are structured as either short 4-6 week modules, or longer 3-12 month programs.

Analyze

We also decide which metrics and overall trends - such as blood glucose, weight, medication adherence - are important to track for each program.

Review

All of our modules and programs undergo a thorough review by our medical staff and members of our Clinical Review Board. In addition, we ensure a fourth grade reading level as measured by industry-standard Flesch-Kincaid scores.

Iterate

Using our built-in analytics engine, we then test our programs with with a group of patients to optimize for many factors, including timing, tonality, frequency and complexity. All programs are approved by our partner organizations prior to launch.
